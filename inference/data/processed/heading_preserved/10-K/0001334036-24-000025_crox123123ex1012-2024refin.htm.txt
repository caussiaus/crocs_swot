2024 REFINANCING AMENDMENT


This REFINANCING AMENDMENT (this “Amendment”), dated as of February 13, 2024, is made by and among Crocs, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Borrower”), the Subsidiary Guarantors party hereto, Citibank, N.A., as Administrative Agent under the Existing Credit Agreement (as defined below) (the “Administrative Agent”) and each of the Lenders party hereto.
PRELIMINARY STATEMENTS:
(1) The Borrower, the Lenders party thereto from time to time and the Administrative Agent are party to that certain Term Loan Credit Agreement, dated as of February 17, 2022 (as amended by the Refinancing Amendment, dated as of August 8, 2023, and as further amended, restated, supplemented, waived or otherwise modified from time to time prior to the date hereof, the “Existing Credit Agreement” and, the Existing Credit Agreement as amended by this Amendment, the “Amended Credit Agreement”).
(2) The Borrower has requested that the 2024 Refinancing Term Lenders (as defined below) provide 2024 Refinancing Term Loans (as defined below) in an aggregate principal amount of $820,000,000.00, as Refinancing Term Loans (as defined in the Existing Credit Agreement) pursuant to Section 2.22(a) of the Existing Credit Agreement.
(3) On the Refinancing Effective Date (as defined below), each Existing Term Lender (as defined below) that shall have executed and delivered a consent to this Amendment substantially in the form of the “Consent to Refinancing Amendment” posted to the Lenders on or about January 16, 2024 (a “2024 Refinancing Term Loan Consent”) indicating the “Cashless Settlement Option” (each such Existing Term Lender, a “2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender”) shall be deemed to have exchanged all of its Existing Term Loans (as defined below) (or such lesser amount as allocated to such 2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender on or prior to the Refinancing Effective Date) for an equal aggregate principal amount of 2024 Refinancing Term Loans and such Lender shall thereafter become a 2024 Refinancing Term Lender (as defined below).
(4) On the Refinancing Effective Date, each person that executes and delivers this Amendment as a 2024 Refinancing Term Lender will make 2024 Refinancing Term Loans to the Borrower in an aggregate principal amount equal to its 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Commitment (as defined below), the proceeds of which will be used by the Borrower to repay in full the outstanding principal amount of Existing Term Loans that are not exchanged for 2024 Refinancing Term Loans (including Loans from Existing Term Lenders that execute and deliver a 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Consent indicating the “Post-Closing Settlement Option” (each such Existing Term Lender, a “2024 Refinancing Term Post-Closing Option Lender”)).
(5) With respect to the 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Commitments (as defined below), Citibank, N.A., Morgan Stanley Senior Funding, Inc., PNC Capital Markets, LLC and HSBC Securities (USA) Inc. will act as joint lead arrangers (in such capacity, the “2024 Refinancing Arrangers”).
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual agreements herein contained and other good and valuable consideration, the sufficiency and receipt of which are hereby acknowledged, and subject to the conditions set forth herein, the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
section 1.Defined Terms. Capitalized terms used but not defined herein shall have the meanings assigned to such terms in the Amended Credit Agreement. In addition, as used in this Amendment, the following terms have the meanings specified:
“2024 Refinancing Term Lender” shall mean a person with a 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Commitment on the Refinancing Effective Date. For the avoidance of doubt, an Existing Term Lender immediately prior to the Refinancing Effective Date may also be a 2024 Refinancing Term Lender.

section 3.Existing Term Loan Refinancing. (a) Subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein, each of the 2024 Refinancing Term Lenders agrees to make 2024 Refinancing Term Loans to the Borrower on the Refinancing Effective Date in a principal amount not to exceed its 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Commitment. The Borrower shall prepay in full all Existing Term Loans of each 2024 Refinancing Term Non-Exchanging Lender (and, to the extent the aggregate principal amount of Existing Term Loans of any 2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender exceeds the 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Exchange Commitment of such 2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender, Existing Term Loans of such 2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender in an aggregate principal amount equal to such excess amount) with the gross proceeds of the 2024 Refinancing Term Loans and other funds available to the Borrower. Unless previously terminated, the 2024 Refinancing Term Loan Commitments shall terminate upon the funding of the 2024 Refinancing Term Loans.
(b) Subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein, each 2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender hereby agrees that all of its Existing Term Loans (or a lesser amount allocated to such 2024 Refinancing Term Cashless Settlement Option Lender by the 2024 Refinancing Arrangers on or prior to the Refinancing Effective Date) will be automatically exchanged for a like principal amount of 2024 Refinancing Term Loans on the Refinancing Effective Date.
